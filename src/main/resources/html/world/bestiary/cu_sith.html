    Name: Cu Sith
    Pronunciation: Coo Sith
    Scientific Name: Canis cusidhus
    Race: Aspect
    Height: 3.5-4.5' (1-1.4 m)
    Associated Elements:

    Etymology: From the draicinni language cu sidhe meaning "dog faerie".

    Characteristics: Cu Siths look much like dog Anthros aside from their green fur. They also have long pointed ears on their heads and long tails. Their eyes are generally a fiery red or orange color. Their hair is usually dark in color, most of the time black.

    Abilities: Before the Cactaclysmic Age, all cu siths drew their power from their patron deity, Ferni. This granted them access to two classes of spells. Osteomancers could call upon spirit magic by utilizing bones as a channeling mechanism. Through osteomancy a cu sith could control skeletons among other things. Hellcallers, on the other hand, used fire and darkness magic to call upon the forces of the underworld. In the Post-Cataclysmic Era, most cu siths practice a wider variety of magics. Some still hold on to their osteomancing or hellcalling roots, but those are practiced less often due to being borderline forbidden arts.

    Habitat: Most cu siths have come to prefer larger villages, towns, or cities as their home. Some of them have a preference for regions of volcanic activity. Others live in seclusion to practice more dubious magical arts.

    Behavior: The living arrangements for cu siths are dependent on the type of magic they practice. Those who do not use magic or use approved forms of magic tend to be more integrated into society. Those who practice the magic of their patron deity tend to be more reclusive. Entire villages of cu sith were not uncommon. They were mostly avoided, however, due to their dire reputation. In general, cu siths tend to be physically strong. In the past they were very rough and tumble and would flaunt their strengths in battle. A cu sith would not be remiss to save the bones from the slain or hunted. They would keep piles of these bones in their homes; although, in more modern times hunters would usually showcase bones from their particularly tough kills in a neater fashion. On a side note, some cu siths will traditionally braid their long and furry tails. The tradition usually depends on family history.

    Diet: Cu sith diets are fairly similar to those of other Anthros and Aspects. They do have a tendency to prefer red meat though.

    Language: Before the creation of the Common language, most cau siths spoke a variety of languages. The most common of these was the language of the draicinni, similar to the Earth language of Old Irish.
