    Name: Peskara
    Pronunciation: PEH-skar-uh
    Scientific Name: Anolis piscosus
    Race: Anthro
    Height: 3-4' (0.8-1.2 m)
    Associated Elements: None

    Etymology: From the peskara language, pescado meaning "fish" and escala meaning "scale".

    Characteristics: Peskaras look mostly like green lizards with blue-green scales over most of their bodies. Their heads feature light blue frills or spines lining the top and back, and long, thick snouts in front. Lighter green scales line the front of their torsos and the undersides of their tails. The defining characteristic of the peskaras are their long, fish-like tails. At the ends of their tails are shark-like fins. Additional fins can be found on their forearms.

    Abilities: Peskaras are adept swimmers. Their lean hydrodynamic bodies, fins, and tail allows them to swim through the water at fast speeds. They are also capable of holding their breath for long periods of time. Peskara claws can be kept rather sharp as well, which are useful for spearing fish or attacking threatening foes in combat.

    Habitat: The peskara population prefers living near rivers, lakes, and seas more than other Anthros. Historically, they prefer small coastal villages. In modern times, they can be found in cities near large bodies of water.

    Behavior: Peskaras are well-known for two things: their sailing ability and their armorsmithing skills. Peskaras are historically known as good sailors. Whether they are piloting small fishing boats, scouting ships, or wartime vessels, peskara sailors are known as some of the best in the world. Peskara armorsmiths, on the other hand, are good at crafting scale mail armor. Techniques for smithing such armor have been passed down among peskara families for generations. Some of their rarest armor utilizes dragon scales. As far as magic goes, they have a greater tendency to study elemental magic over the arcane or metaphysical. Despite the fact that they are clearly aquatic in nature, the peskaras believe they are descended from primordial dragons.

    Diet: Being mostly coastal, they have a preference for shellfish. However, they aren't against eating fruit, small insects, or a normal Anthro diet as well.

    Language: Peskaras used to speak their own language, which is similar to that of Earth Portuguese. As with many Anthros, they adopted the Common language around the time of its creation.
