    Name: Ratatosk
    Pronunciation: rat-uh-TOSK
    Scientific Name: Sciurus medeis
    Classification: Monster
    Height: 6-10" (0.15-0.25 m)
    Threat Level: Moderately Low
    Associated Elements:

    Etymology: From the Drekaldr language Ratatoskr.

    Characteristics: Ratatosks look like typical red squirrels. Strangely enough, though, their tails transition from fur at the base to long green vine-like strands at the ends. They are infused with natural magics, giving them this odd appearance. Using their magical abilities, these vine-like appendages are prehensile and extensible.

    Habitat: Ratatosks only live in the Snowscape, the frigid northern area of Casarok. They tend to be found in and around conifer forests or wherever there may be trees.

    Behavior: Ratatosks all live in trees as most squirrels do. They can often be found running up and down their home trees foraging for and storing their food. They are strictly plant eaters, preferring to eat the few nuts, seeds, and berries that exist in the low temperature areas they live in. They are physically fairly harmless aside from their sharp, frontal teeth. The only real threat they pose to others is in their ability to use nature magic. Most ratatosks will use minor spells to defend themselves. Some ratatosks have mischievous natures and will engage in trickery with anthros, ferals, and other monsters. They will cause others to trip with roots, wrap them up in vines, or transform them entirely on rare occasions. The reason for this sneaky behavior is unknown, but many scientists theorize it is to make their presence known.

    Interactions: Ratatosks are coveted by some northern peoples for their cute qualities. They are hunted by others for their thick fur. Their fur is often made into warm gloves where this is the case. More often than not, they are left alone unless they engage in some form of trickery with someone nearby.
