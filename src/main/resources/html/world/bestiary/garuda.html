    Name: Garuda
    Pronunciation: guh-roo-DUH
    Scientific Name: Aquilla garudae
    Race: Aspect
    Height: 3.5-4.5' (1-1.4 m)
    Associated Elements:

    Etymology: From a southern Casarokan language, garuda meaning "devourer".

    Characteristics: Garudas are typically similar in color and appearance to an eagle or kite Anthro. Their feathers range from white to red to brown in color. Like most winged Aspects, their wings are on their backs. Their forearms and hands are similar to their shins and feet, scaly with talons, as opposed to avian Anthros who have wing-like arms.

    Abilities: Before the Cactaclysmic Age, all garudas gained their powers from the deity Stratos. This power granted them access to various air and cloud magics. They could even create clouds that one could stand upon. Aside from their main focus, they could learn various wind, light, and frost spells.

    Habitat: Garudas live all over Casarok. Their population is very spread out from as far west as the Acaraho Mountains to the farthest eastern reaches of the world. They also inhabit the northern and southern mountains as well. They have a preference for higher elevations and tend to stick close to the mountain ranges in those regions. Strangely enough, they are rare to find in central Casarok. Furthermore, they are known to live in the cloudlands.

    Behavior: Garudas have a natural love of the sky. It is not uncommon for places where garudas live to have tall towers and structures. They can also be seen flying in the areas where they live. In places where many garudas gather, they create cloudlands high in the sky. The practice of creating cloudlands was frowned upon more and more as time went on, however, due to the perpetual shadows they cast upon the ground. In modern times, creating new cloudlands is nearly forbidden unless special permission is granted from the local government. Regardless, many garudas have a preference for living in these cloudlands. Aside from their choice of living establishment, garudas are fairly friendly with other anthros in Casarok. They tend to be quite inviting and will even offer a lift on their backs should the need arise for a short flight. In times of war through history, garudas have a preference for lances and bows. Garudas are known throughout Casarok to be some of the most skilled archers in the world.

    Diet: The diet of a garuda depends on the region he or she is from. They enjoy various foods that are grown only in the cloudlands. Those that live in the mountains or by the sea tend to favor local fish.

    Language: Garudas never spoke a single language in their history. They always spoke the languages local to their population. Eventually, as with all of Casarok, they picked up the Common language.
