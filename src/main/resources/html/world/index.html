    Welcome to the world of Casarok

    Casarok is the homeworld of Chase and his friends. This world exists far from the planet Earth, and it is currently unknown whether or not this planet is even located in the same universe. It shares many similarities with Earth such as its composition, development, and diversity of life; however, magic runs deep within the planet and its inhabitants. The world has a large pantheon of gods and goddesses as well.
