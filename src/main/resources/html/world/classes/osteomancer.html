    Name: Osteomancer
    Pronunciation: AHS-tee-oh-man-ser
    Associated Elements:

    The osteomancer class refers to those who use spirit magic by utilizing bones as a magical focus. This practice gives them unique abilities that are otherwise not possible with normal spirit magic. The practice of osteomancy usually centers around bones and skeletons. These abilities can be used both offensively and defensively, granting them a lot of utility in combat. Some common ostoemancy spells are listed below.

    • Bone Blast: Fires a blast of spirit energy in the shape of a spinning bone toward an enemy target.
    • Bone Shield: Grants the target a shield of spirit energy in the shape of bones, providing defense against magical attacks.
    • Harden Body: Hardens the target's body from their skeleton outward, granting them increased defense against physical attacks.
    • Empower Claws: Hardens and sharpens the claws of the target, increasing their offensive power.
    • Jointlock: Harden the joints of a target, slowing them. Powerful osteomancers can completely lock down a target, freezing them in place.
    • Puppet Skeleton: Channel spirit energy to take control of a target, granting the ability to control them as if they were a puppet. Can be resisted by targets that have a will of their own.

    Due to the nature of their ability to manipulate and control skeletons, osteomancy is often confused for necromancy by those who do not have the knowledge to tell the difference. Osteomancy as a whole has therefore become a near forbidden art in modern times by the International Council of Magic. Some cu siths advocate for the use of osteomancy, which keeps it from being forbidden, but it is a topic of heavy debate. Modern osteomancers are still forced to practice the art in secret.
