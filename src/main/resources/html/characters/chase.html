    Hey, folks! How's it going?

    Full name: Chase l'Orange
    Species: Striped Skunk (Mephitis mephitis)
    Gender: Male
    Age: late 20s
    Height: 4' 0" (1.22 m)
    Occupation: Generalist
    Favorite Color: Orange
    Favorite Food: Fruit based confections, particularly creamsicles
    Likes: games, chilling out, transformation
    Dislikes: surprises, overworking, people disrespecting others

    Physical Traits: Chase's fur has a bright orange hue to it, an oddity among his species. His ears are also slightly larger than that of a typical skunk as well. Additionally, his scent is that of oranges and cream rather than the odor often produced by those of his species. Regardless of all the odd traits he possesses, he bears all the normal markings one would expect such as the stripes leading from his nose and down his back to the tip of his tail. Chase's choice of clothing often reflects his easygoing attitude. Regularly one will find him wearing his light blue Hawaiian shirt with the flower and palm tree print. Once in a while, he will also wear his shades.

    Character Traits: Chase is generally a cheerful individual. A smile lighting up his bright orange muzzle or a curious twitch of his oversized ears can be a soothing sight to others. He will joke around and be playful with his friends, though sometimes he takes his games a bit too far. He is also very easygoing, appearing to be loose and lax most of the time; Chase rarely seems to have a problem chilling out at any given moment. His relaxed nature tends be his biggest downfall, however. He never expects anything to go wrong and is often prone to being surprised. He also becomes lazy on occasion too, occasionally at the wrong time. Nevertheless, he has a sense of loyalty to others. He will go so far as to put himself in harm's way to protect someone who is being bullied. Chase also hates to offend people, so much so that he changed his scent magically to prevent his odor from causing a problem.

    Abilities: Chase has the ability to shapeshift at will into practically any form he can imagine. He can also change the forms of other people by flowing his magic energy into someone via physical contact or forcing the energy through a wave of magic, for example in the form of the "Magic Zap" or "Ha-dook-en". His magic has some limits as he is unable to change specific aspects of one's body like his height or weight unless its characteristic of the new form.

    Skills: Chase has a knack for working with computers. Through his shapeshifting abilities, he has also improved his knowledge of different lifeforms.

    Background: Not much is known about the circumstances of his birth. Chase was found as an orphan by his vulpine foster parents, Paul and Vivien l'Orange. When the fox couple found him, he was in the form of a fox kit with a mysterious chrysolite pendant softly wrapped with care among his fur. His birth parents have not been found to this day, and he has no known blood relatives at this time.

    Chase was raised as the lone child of the l'Orange family and maintained his identity as a red fox. At an early age, he and his parents discovered the powers held within the pendant with which he was found. The magic within in the strange talisman allowed him to utilize transformation magic. He could not only shapeshift at will, but he could also focus his energy to change the form of another. It took him a great deal of time and effort to control this magic power. Various mishaps happened along the way that landed him in trouble as a child. Some issues caused his family to become a source of outrage in their community while other instances forced them out of town violently.

    When Chase turned 18, he left his parents home for fear of causing them further problems and went off to college. While working on a degree in information technology, he would continue to test and master his magical abilities in secret. Eventually, he was able to gain greater control over his magic. Chase ended up using his shapeshifting abilities to help earn his way through school. Once he graduated from college, he continued to use his unique magical powers to do odd jobs (with the occasional computer job thrown in just to make use of his degree.) He met several of his friends this way and still often takes on tasks as Malichi's bodyguard, Katrina's subordinate, and Ruby's expedition assistant.

    In 2015, Chase came across an old artifact belonging to Ruby that was said to reveal one's true identity. Upon glancing into the mirror he learned of his true form as a skunk, having believed his entire life that he was a fox.

    No one seems to know the origin of Chase's strange coloration and his bloodline seems to be lost in history; but is there some deeper meaning behind his appearance and powers...?
